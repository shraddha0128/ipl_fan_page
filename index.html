Ruturaj Gaikwad – The New Captain

Ruturaj Gaikwad, a calm and focused batsman, has emerged as one of CSK’s brightest talents. Now leading the team with pride, Ruturaj represents the next-gen CSK legacy.

  • Debut for CSK: 2020
  • Orange Cap Winner – IPL 2021
  • Known for elegance and consistency
  • Captaincy Style: Calm, strategic, and inspiring
  • Often compared to Dhoni for his composed approach
